# 🛒 Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze customer shopping behavior and uncover key insights related to revenue trends, customer demographics, and purchasing patterns to support better business decision-making.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used

* **Python** (Pandas, Data Cleaning)
* **SQL** (Data querying and analysis)
* **Power BI** (Interactive dashboard and visualization)

---

## 📊 Dashboard Overview

This interactive dashboard provides a comprehensive view of customer shopping behavior using key performance indicators and visual insights.

### 🔑 Key Metrics

* **Total Customers:** 646
* **Total Revenue:** $39.07K
* **Average Order Value:** $60.48
* **Average Rating:** 3.77

---

## 📈 Key Visualizations

* **Revenue by Gender**
  Displays contribution of male and female customers to overall revenue.

* **Revenue by Category**
  Highlights top-performing product categories such as Clothing, Accessories, Footwear, and Outerwear.

* **Payment Method Usage**
  Shows distribution of different payment methods used by customers.

* **Revenue by Age Group**
  Analyzes spending patterns across different age groups (Young Adults, Adults, Middle Aged, Seniors).

* **Filters (Slicers)**

  * Subscription Status
  * Gender
  * Product Category
  * Shipping Type

---

## 💡 Key Insights

* **Clothing category generates the highest revenue**, making it the most profitable segment.
* **Male customers contribute a larger share of revenue (~70%)** compared to female customers.
* **Senior and middle-aged customers are the highest spenders**, indicating strong purchasing power.
* **Digital payment methods are widely used**, showing customer preference for convenience.
* **Average order value of $60.48** suggests moderate customer spending per transaction.
